Farmer’s Cinnamon Muffins
Ingredients
  • 2 cups Farmer’s Breakfast Multi-purpose Mix
  • ⅓ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseeds (milled linseeds)
  • ¼ cup old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  2. Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the water and flaxseeds. Set aside to allow it to gel.
  4. In a separate small bowl, mix together the cinnamon crumb topping. Set aside.
  5. In a large bowl, mix together the flours, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Make a well in it, and add almond milk, coconut oil, vinegar, and flax egg.
  6. Mix everything together using a fork, or a hand beater on low speed, until all the ingredients are just combined.
  7. Evenly divide the batter into the muffin cups until ½ full. Evenly top with the cinnamon crumb topping.
  8. Bake in the oven for about 16-18 minutes,
  9. Allow to slightly cool in the tin, then transfer to a wire rack to finish cooling.
  10. Serve warm or room temperature.

Farmer’s Pancakes
Ingredients
  • 2 cups Farmer’s Breakfast Multi-purpose Mix
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Directions
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix Farmer’s Breakfast and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside.
  3. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  4. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.
  5. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.
  6. Ladle batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  7. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Farmers Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies
Ingredients
  • 1 ½ cup Farmer’s Breakfast Multi-purpose Mix
  • ½ cup (8oz, 2 sticks) salted butter softened
  • ½ cup peanut butter creamy or chunky
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup quick-cooking oats*
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
Directions
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F. Line three baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats.
  2. In a large bowl or the bowl of your stand mixer, combine butter, peanut butter, and sugar. Beat until well-combined and fluffy, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla. Beat until completely mixed.
  4. Add Farmers Breakfast mix and oats.. Beat (if using a hand-held mixer or making by hand, use a wooden spoon to stir) until just mixed and a sticky dough forms.
  5. Scoop golf-ball-sized scoops of batter onto baking sheets, 8 to a sheet as far apart as possible
  6. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until cookies are browned around the edges and tops look mostly set.
  7. Allow cookies to cool on baking tray. Enjoy!
